TBSI Winter Data Science Workshop (TBSI-WODS)

Date:Dec 17-19, 2019

Invited Talks:
The talks would comprise any topic pertaining to the domain of Data Science. For the 3-day workshop, we would like to invite guest speakers and TBSI faculty for the following events:

  • Keynote speeches (60-90min)*
  • Invited talks (40min)*

To become a speaker:
What to Submit

  • Brief abstract containing the title of the talk and its description.
  • Short biography of yourself.

Note: Travel and accommodation for invited speakers will be funded by TBSI. And be prepared for a Q&A section during the final 10 minutes

TBSI

Tsinghua-Berkeley Shenzhen institute (TBSI) is jointly established in 2015 by Tsinghua University and University of California, Berkeley under the support of the Shenzhen Municipal Government. Its mission is to pursue interdisciplinary research, international education and industrial partnership, and it is committed to cultivating collaboration between academia, industry and government.
