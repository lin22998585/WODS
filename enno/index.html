TBSI Workshop on Data Science (TBSI-WODS)


Date: Dec 17-19, 2019

The first annual TBSI Workshop on Data Science aims to create a platform for frontier researchers in the field of data science to share their cutting-edge ideas on open problems and to facilitate the fostering of research collaborations amongst them and students. The workshop will comprise of seminars on research highlights and tutorials by the invited speakers. Additionally, there will be competitions for students to compete in with their research presentations and creative ideas and stand a chance to win fabulous prizes.

TBSI

Tsinghua-Berkeley Shenzhen institute (TBSI) is jointly established in 2015 by Tsinghua University and University of California, Berkeley under the support of the Shenzhen Municipal Government. Its mission is to pursue interdisciplinary research, international education and industrial partnership, and it is committed to cultivating collaboration between academia, industry and government.
